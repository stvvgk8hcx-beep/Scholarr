Calendar Export

Export your academic schedule to your preferred calendar app.

ICS files can be imported into any calendar app. In Apple Calendar: File → Import. In Google Calendar: Settings → Import. In Outlook: File → Open & Export → Import/Export.

Cloud Backup

To use cloud backup, export your data as a JSON file and upload it to your preferred cloud storage.

🔵 Google Drive
Export your data then upload to Google Drive. Open drive.google.com to manage backups.
🟦 OneDrive
Export your data then upload to OneDrive. Open onedrive.live.com to manage backups.
☁️ iCloud Drive
Export your data then upload to iCloud Drive. Open icloud.com/iclouddrive to manage backups.
